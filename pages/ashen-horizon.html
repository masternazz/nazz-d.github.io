Ashen Horizon

A mostly-vanilla Minecraft 1.26.2 modpack built for immersion and exploration. Other players reach back into their backpack when they open inventory. You hear friends before you see them. Desolate wasteland biomes stretch to a horizon rendered 128 chunks out with shaders. Storage evolves from a leather pack to a full digital network.

Immersion Layer

  • Not Enough Animations — when a player opens their inventory, their arm reaches back like they're pulling from a pack on their back. Map-holding, spyglass, shield, oar — all visible in third person.
  • Traveler's Backpack — physical 3D backpack renders on the player model, so the reach-back animation has something to reach into.
  • Better Animations Collection — overhauled mob and player model animations (updated May 2026 for 1.26.2).
  • Fresh Animations resource pack (requires EMF + ETF mods) — 60+ mobs fully reanimated: cows bob, creepers sway, villagers gesture.
  • Customizable Player Models — full custom 3D player model editor, stored in your Minecraft skin.
  • First Person Model — see your own body, arms, and backpack in first person.

Voice & Sound

  • Simple Voice Chat — proximity voice chat with directional 3D audio. Hear people get louder as they approach, fade as they walk away.
  • Walkie Talkie addon — craft a radio item, set a channel, talk across any distance. Good for squad comms or a dedicated base channel.
  • AmbientSounds 5 — dynamic ambient audio: caves echo, wind blows in open fields, forests are alive at night.
  • Presence Footsteps — per-surface footstep sounds. Grass, gravel, wood, snow, stone all sound different underfoot.
  • Sound Physics Remastered — reverb in caves, muffled audio through walls, sounds bounce off surfaces. Pairs directly with voice chat so player voices echo in caverns.
Storage

Storage Arc

Day one: craft a Traveler's Backpack — it equips to your back and is visible to other players. Week one: Sophisticated Backpacks with upgrade slots (auto-pickup, smelting, magnet, void junk). Mid-game: Sophisticated Storage chests with the same module system. Endgame: Refined Storage — confirmed on Fabric 1.26.2 — digital network with one terminal for everything. Carry On lets you pick up chests and mobs with bare hands anytime.

Terrain

Waste Zones

Three terrain mods stack together: Terralith adds 100+ biomes including Fractured Savanna, Arid Highlands, Volcanic Peaks, and Scarlet Mountains — the main wasteland sources. Tectonic reshapes large-scale geography: mountain ridges run thousands of blocks, ocean trenches plunge deep, canyons form naturally. Geophilic adds detail inside vanilla biomes — fallen logs, boulders, clearings, mossy stumps. All three are compatible and stack cleanly.

Visuals

Shaders + Distant Horizons

Iris Shaders + Distant Horizons 2.3 together: normal render distance at 16 chunks, LOD terrain rendered out to 128 chunks in simplified geometry with shaders applied. Four supported shader packs — IterationT (confirmed on 1.26.2, planetary atmosphere simulation), Complementary Reimagined, Rethinking Voxels, and BSL. Two optional theme swaps in the resourcepacks folder: gothic dark and futuristic sci-fi, off by default.

Infrastructure

The Ashen Horizon server runs inside a Docker container managed by Pelican Wings on LXC 121 (VLAN 2, Game Servers). DNS is a Cloudflare SRV record — _minecraft._tcp.ashenhorizon.masternazz.com — so players connect to ashenhorizon.masternazz.com with no port needed. Simple Voice Chat uses a separate UDP port forward (24454) already configured in OPNsense. Modpack files are hosted on self-hosted Nextcloud at cloud.masternazz.com. The Discord gamebot tracks server status, player counts via RCON, and handles auto-stop after 20 minutes idle.
